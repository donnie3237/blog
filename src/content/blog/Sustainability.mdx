## What is it?

Sustainability refers to managing resources in a way that meets current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It focuses on integrating economic, social, and environmental aspects to achieve a balanced and sustainable development.

## Sustainable Practices

* 1\. Responsible Consumption

Reducing unnecessary resource consumption and choosing sustainable products, such as recyclable products or those made from sustainable materials, can help lessen environmental impact.

* 2\. Using Renewable Energy

Utilizing clean and renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydro power can reduce greenhouse gas emissions and help combat global warming.

* 3\. Reducing Waste

Reusing and recycling waste can help decrease the amount of waste that needs to be disposed of and reduce the use of natural resources. Opting for biodegradable packaging or reusing materials can effectively reduce waste.

* 4\. Conserving Natural Resources

Protecting and restoring natural environments, such as forests, rivers, and oceans, is essential for maintaining sustainability. Supporting conservation projects and participating in tree planting can positively impact the environment.

* Building Sustainable Communities

Promoting strong and sustainable communities through quality education, stable employment, and social support helps communities grow sustainably and face various challenge
